How to post a shift in Shift Manager?

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from administrations. If your credentials are wrong or you forgot them, please contact the administration team.

  2. From the right side menu, select Shift Manager options.

  3. Click on the Post Shift button on the right side of the page.

  4. You will see a pop-up where you are required to fill in the details related to the shift.

  5. Once all the details are filled in, click on the Save button at the bottom.
