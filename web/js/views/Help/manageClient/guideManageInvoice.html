Manage invoice

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from administrations. If your credentials are wrong or you forgot them, please contact the administration team.

  2. Go to the side menu and click on Invoice Manager.

  3. Select Published Invoice to see pending invoices.

  4. When the administrator publishes an invoice, you will see it under published invoices.

  5. Click on the eye icon to view the invoice.

  6. You can scroll and see all the details about the amount there. You have the option to download the invoice as well. Once you make the payment, make sure to click on the Invoice Pay button at the bottom.

  7. Wait until the payment is processed.

  8. When the invoice is approved by the administrator, you will be able to see the info under the Paid Invoices menu section

  9. You can also view the invoice and download it by clicking on the eyeball icon.
