How to register a new account?

  1. Click on the Create an account link at the bottom of the login page.

  2. Fill in the required details accordingly. You can find your address by inputting the postal code and clicking on the Find My Address button.

  3. Select the correct address from the dropdown and the address info will be displayed.

  4. Once all the details have been entered, click on the Create Account button at the bottom of the page.

  5. The login credentials will be sent to the registered email address inbox.
