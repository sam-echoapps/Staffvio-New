Staff Education

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from administrations.

  2. From the right side menu, Click on “Education”

  3. Now, click on the 'Add Education' button.

    4. Now, enter the candidate's education details and click on the 'Save' button.

  4. A pop-up confirming the successful addition of education details will appear. Click 'OK'

  5. You have successfully added your details.

  6. If there is any mistake, you can edit it using the 'Edit' option in the 'Action' menu.
