Right To Work

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from administrations

  2. From the right side menu, Select “Right To Work”

  3. Now, the candidate should enter passport and visa details.

  4. Check the details you provided to ensure correctness, and then click on 'Save'.

  5. A pop-up confirming the successful addition of right-to-work details will appear. Click 'OK'
