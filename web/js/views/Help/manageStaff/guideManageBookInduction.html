Book Induction

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from the administration.

  2. From the right side menu, select Book Induction.

  3. After selecting the date and time slot for induction training, click on the 'Submit' button.

  4. Wait for the administrator to confirm the induction booking you made.

  5. Once the induction booking is confirmed, you will be notified of the same as well. The team will contact you to share the meeting details.

  6. After attending the induction, your status will be changed to attended. Click on the Click here link to submit the induction checklist form.

  7. After filling out the list, scroll down to the bottom of the page and click Submit.

  8. Once the checklist is submitted, the administrator will confirm it.
