Staff Next Of Kin

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from administrations.

  2. From the right side menu, Click on “Next of Kin”

  3. Provide the candidates “ Next of Kin” details.

  4. Now, click on 'Save' after verifying that the details you provided are correct.

  5. A pop-up confirming the save of Next of Kin details will appear. Click 'OK'.
