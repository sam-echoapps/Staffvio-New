Contract

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from the administration.

  2. From the right side menu, Select Contract.

  3. If the contract has not been shared with you then you will see something like below.

  4. Once the administrator shares the contract, you will be able to view it.

  5. Read the contract and scroll down to the bottom of the page. Fill in the declaration details and click on the submit button.

  6. Wait for the administrator to approve the contract.

  7. Once the contract is approved by the administrator, you will be notified via email and will be able to see it in the profile.
