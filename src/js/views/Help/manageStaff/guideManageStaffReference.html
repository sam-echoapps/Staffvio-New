Staff Reference

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from administrations.

  2. From the right side menu, select References.

  3. Now, click on the 'Add Reference' button.

  4. In the reference section, provide the names and contact details of two references, and then click on the 'Save' button.

  5. After entering the references, click on the 'OK' button.

  6. In the Action menu, click on the 'Send Email' button.

  7. A pop-up message for sending the referral form will appear. Click on the 'Yes' button as the information you provided is correct.

  8. Click 'OK,' as a pop-up confirming the successful sending of the email will appear.

  9. In the Action menu, click on 'Upload.

  10. Now, upload the reference document.

  11. The referrer status will change to 'Received,' and now click on the document preview to view your document.

  12. After checking, click the 'Close' button.
