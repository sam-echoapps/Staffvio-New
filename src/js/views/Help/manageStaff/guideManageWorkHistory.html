Work History

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from administrations.

  2. From the right side menu, select Work History

  3. Click on the 'Add Work History' option.

    4. Enter the details, attach the certificate to the work history form, and click the 'Save' button.

  4. Successfully added your details. Now click 'OK

  5. If there is any mistake, you can edit it using the 'Edit' option in the 'Action' menu.
