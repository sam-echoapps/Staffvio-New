DBS

  1. Login into your account with the credentials you got from administrations.

  2. From the right side menu, Select “DBS”.

  3. The candidate should update whether they have a current DBS certificate.

  4. Update that you have a certificate by clicking 'Yes' in the dropdown.

  5. Now, upload the certificate you have so that the administration can arrange training for any missing certificates.

  6. Click on “Save”.

  7. A pop-up confirming the successful addition of DBS details will appear. Click 'OK'
